## Animation Details

### Orb Animations
1. **Idle**: Gentle sine wave pulse (0.05 amplitude)
2. **Listening**: 
   - Bass waves (0.3 amplitude)
   - Mid waves (0.2 amplitude)
   - Treble waves (0.15 amplitude)
3. **Processing**: Spinning patterns + glow
4. **Speaking**: Output frequency waves

### Rotation
- Continuous slow rotation
- Y-axis: 0.002 rad/frame
- X-axis: 0.001 rad/frame

### Color Transitions
- Smooth interpolation between states
- Fresnel glow effect on edges
- Pulsing brightness (sin wave)
